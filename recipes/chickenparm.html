Chicken Parmesan

Description

This recipe will teach you how to make oven-baked chicken parmesan with a side of pasta. It does not take long to prepare and is a healthy, delicious dish. Additionally, there are only 4 required ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 2lbs Boneless Skinless Chicken Breast
  • 1 8oz. bag of Shredded Mozzerella Cheese
  • 2 16oz. jars of Pasta Sauce
  • 1 box of Pasta
  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Depending on the thickness of the chicken, filet breasts down to no greater than 1 inch in thickness.
  3. Once oven is preheated, add chicken to a baking dish and bake in the oven for 15 minutes.
  4. While the chicken is baking, bring 4 quarts of water to a boil on the stovetop.
  5. Add the box of pasta to the water and boil for 7-9 minutes, periodically testing it for desired texture.
  6. After pasta is finished, remove the water by straining it in the sink. Set aside.
  7. Remove the baked chicken from the oven and add 2 jars of sauce to the dish, and then evenly distribute the cheese on top.
  8. Place the dish back in the oven for up to 2 minutes, or until cheese is completely melted.
  9. Remove the chicken parmesan from the oven and serve with pasta.
