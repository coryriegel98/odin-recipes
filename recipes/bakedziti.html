Baked Ziti

Description

This recipe will teach you how to make baked ziti. It does not take long to prepare and is a healthy, delicious dish. Additionally, there are only 4 required ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1lb of Ground Sausage
  • 1 8oz. bag of Shredded Mozzerella Cheese
  • 2 16oz. jars of Pasta Sauce
  • 1 box of Ziti
  1. Heat a pan to medium heat and add ground sausage to the pan.
  2. Cook the ground sausage until it is lightly browned and set aside.
  3. Bring 4 quarts of water to a boil on the stovetop.
  4. Add the box of pasta to the water and boil for 7-9 minutes, periodically testing it for desired texture.
  5. After ziti is finished, remove the water by straining it in the sink. Set aside.
  6. Add the sausage, ziti, and pasta sauce into a baking dish and combine well. Once mixed, evenly distribute the cheese on top.
  7. Place the dish back in the oven for up to 15 minutes
  8. Remove the baked ziti from the oven and serve.
